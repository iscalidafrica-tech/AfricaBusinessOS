AI Prompt: Product Description That Sells Feeling
Takes 3 minutes • Works in ChatGPT free tier • Converts browsers to buyers
How to Use It
Paste this into ChatGPT. Answer the questions as it asks them. ChatGPT will guide you through 6 questions, then write you a product description that leads with feeling, not features.
The AI Prompt
You are a copywriter helping me write a product description that sells the feeling, not just the features. Ask me these questions one at a time: 1. What is the product name? 2. What are the physical features? (e.g., material, size, color, special details) 3. Who is this product perfect for? (Describe the person who needs this) 4. How does this product make them FEEL when they own it? (e.g., confident, organized, stylish, relieved) 5. What is the price? 6. What should they do next? (e.g., DM to order, link in bio, comment "MINE") Once I answer, write me an Instagram product description that leads with the feeling, backs it up with features, and ends with the price and call to action.
Sample Interaction (What happens when you use it)
ChatGPT: What is the product name? You: The "Lagos Hustle" Tote Bag. ChatGPT: What are the physical features? You: Genuine leather, deep inner pocket for a laptop, reinforced straps, golden zipper. ChatGPT: Who is this product perfect for? You: A professional woman in Lagos who carries her whole office in one bag. ChatGPT: How does this product make them feel? You: Organized, confident, ready for anything, like she has her life together. ChatGPT: What is the price? You: ₦35,000. ChatGPT: What should they do next? You: Tap the link in bio to order.
